Foundation


Olanto is a not-for-profit Foundation managed under the Swiss law. Its headquarters are in Geneva, Switzerland but the Foundation is placed under the responsibility of the Swiss Confederation, to which it ultimately reports.

Olanto is managed by a Foundation Board, which is helped by a Support Committee and a Committee of Experts.

Olanto aims at creating and distributing free software in the field of Computer-Assisted Translation (CAT), Machine Translation (MT), multilingual search and other language-related fields. Olanto is an acronym which stands for "Open Language Tools".

The Committee of Experts

The Committee of Experts advises the Foundation Board on technical issues.

It may for example propose to add to modules to the suite of CAT tools, or to modify or replace an existing tool. It can also contribute to setting up the debugging and upgrading priorities. While its advice is non-biding for the Foundation Board, it is generally heard because its membership is composed of highly-skilled technicians.

The Support Committee

The Support Committee advises the Foundation Board on strategic, financial and other matters.

It may include representatives of various stakeholders such as the Swiss Confederation, customers, the academic world, the open source world, etc. Its advice is also non-biding for the Foundation Board.
